Butter Chicken

Prep Time: 40 mins | Difficulty: Easy | Type: Non-Veg

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Boneless, skinless chicken breast, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 medium Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup Tomato sauce or puree
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream (or plain yogurt for a lighter option)
  • 1 tbsp Garam masala
  • 1 tsp Mild chili powder or paprika
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil (olive or any cooking oil)

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes until they are browned on all sides. Remove the chicken from the pan and set it aside on a plate.
  2. In the same pan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the finely chopped onion and cook for 3-4 minutes until it becomes soft and golden.
  3. Stir in the garam masala and chili powder. Let the spices cook for about 1 minute—it will smell amazing!
  4. Pour the tomato sauce into the pan and stir well. Let the sauce bubble and simmer for 10 minutes so it thickens up slightly.
  5. Turn the heat down to low. Slowly stir in the heavy cream (or yogurt) until the sauce becomes a beautiful rich orange color.
  6. Put the cooked chicken back into the pan. Let everything simmer gently for 5 more minutes so the chicken absorbs the flavors.
  7. Serve hot with warm naan bread or over a bowl of plain rice.
