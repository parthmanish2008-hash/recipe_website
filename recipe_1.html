Red Sauce Pasta

Prep Time: 20 mins | Difficulty: Easy | Type: Veg

Ingredients

  • 450 - 500gPasta
  • 2 tbsp oil(olive or any cooking oil)
  • 3 cloves Garlic, cut into slices
  • 10 - 12 medium sized tomatoes , crushed
  • 1 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp Red pepper flakes (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • Cheese (Parmesan or any preferred cheese)

Instructions

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the chopped garlic and saute for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  3. Pour in the crushed tomatoes, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper. Stir well.
  4. Lower the heat and let the sauce simmer for 10-15 minutes so the flavors blend together.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta into the red sauce until it is completely coated.
  6. Serve hot, garnished with fresh coriander and a sprinkle of Cheese.
