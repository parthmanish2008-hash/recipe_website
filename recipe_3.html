Chocolate Cake

Prep Time: 1 hr 15 mins | Difficulty: Hard | Type: Dessert

Ingredients

  • 2 cups All-purpose flour
  • 2 cups Sugar
  • 3/4 cup Unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp Baking soda
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Vegetable oil
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 cup Hot water (this is the secret to a super soft, moist cake!)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease two 9-inch round baking pans with a little butter or oil.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add the milk, vegetable oil, eggs, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix everything together on medium speed until well combined.
  4. Carefully pour in the hot water and stir gently. Don't worry if the batter looks very thin and watery—this is exactly how it should look!
  5. Pour the batter evenly into your two prepared baking pans.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes. To check if it's done, poke the center with a toothpick; if it comes out clean, the cake is ready.
  7. Let the cakes cool completely before taking them out of the pans and adding your favorite frosting.
